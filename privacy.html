Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Naismith ("the App") is a fantasy basketball companion app that provides real-time NBA injury alerts and waiver wire pickup suggestions. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Device Identifier — When you first open the App, a random unique identifier is generated and stored locally on your device. This identifier is used to associate your roster with your device. It is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information.

Fantasy Roster Data — If you choose to import your Yahoo Fantasy roster or manually add players, that roster data is stored on our servers and associated with your device identifier. This data is used solely to personalize your injury alerts.

Push Notification Token — If you enable push notifications, your device's push notification token is sent to our server so we can deliver real-time alerts. This token is provided by Apple and does not contain personal information.

Crash Reports — The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports. These reports help us identify and fix bugs. They do not contain personal information.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not collect any financial or payment information.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any user data to third parties.
  • We do not serve ads or use any advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — for push notifications
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — for anonymous crash reporting

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Your roster data is stored on our servers as long as you use the App. If you clear your roster within the App, the data is deleted from our servers. Device identifiers and push tokens that have been inactive for more than 90 days are automatically removed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
